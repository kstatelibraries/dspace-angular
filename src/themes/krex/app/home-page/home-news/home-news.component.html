K-State Research Exchange (K-REx)

The K-State Research Exchange (K-REx) provides a platform to collect, preserve, and discover the creative and scholarly works of K-State students, staff, and faculty;

it also houses K-State's digitized historical documents and images from the University Archives and Special Collections. K-REx raises the prestige of K-State's scholarly output, increases scholarly communication, and makes materials more widely available.

If you are a member of the K-State community we invite you to deposit your works to make your impact on the Wildcat Way today.
